// 08 — FAQ

Got Questions?

Who can participate in Inforia Tech-Summit 2K26 Hackathon? +
All currently enrolled college students can participate, including 1st year, 2nd year, 3rd year, and final year students. Students from any branch are welcome.
What should be the team size? +
Each team must have a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 members. Inter-branch teams are allowed.
Can first-year students participate even without technical experience? +
Yes. First-year students are strongly encouraged to participate. A strong idea, clear thinking, and teamwork matter as much as technical skills.
Is coding compulsory to participate? +
No. Coding is not compulsory in Round 1. Teams can participate with a strong idea and presentation. However, shortlisted teams should present a prototype or proof of concept in the offline round.
How will teams be shortlisted for the offline round? +
Submissions were reviewed based on innovation, feasibility, relevance of problem, clarity of presentation, and practical impact.
What happens in the Grand Finale? +
Shortlisted teams will present their prototype or working model before the judging panel on 1st April 2026 at ICOT Seminar Hall, IES College of Technology, Bhopal (09:00 AM – 04:00 PM).
Is a fully completed project required in the final round? +
No. A complete product is not necessary. A working prototype, partial implementation, or clear proof of concept is acceptable.
Will certificates be provided? +
Yes. Certificates will be provided to participants according to event guidelines.
How can participants contact organizers for doubts? +
Contact Saurabh Kumar (IES President) at +91 7991153691, or reach out through the official WhatsApp group.
